NEP 2020

Foster Kids School, Bhirr Pacheri Buhana proudly has a team of NEP 2020 certified teachers dedicated to providing innovative and quality education.🌟s

Foster Kids, one of India's No.1 play school brands, has thoughtfully designed its curriculum in complete alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. The policy emphasizes the Foundational Stage (ages 3–8) as the most critical phase of a child's development, and Foster Kids has seamlessly integrated these principles into its teaching-learning practices.

Our curriculum focuses on experiential, play-based, and activity-driven learning, encouraging curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving skills among young learners. It blends art, music, movement, storytelling, and hands-on exploration to make learning joyful and meaningful. The program also nurtures essential early literacy, numeracy, and socio-emotional skills in a stress-free environment.

In accordance with NEP 2020, Foster Kids promotes multilingual exposure, inclusivity, and the integration of Indian values with global perspectives. Teachers are trained to create a flexible, child-centred classroom where every child learns at their own pace.

Through this NEP-aligned approach, Foster Kids ensures that children receive a strong foundation for lifelong learning, preparing them for future academic success while fostering confidence, empathy, and independence. Foster Kids truly represents the spirit of NEP 2020 — education rooted in play, growth, and joyful discovery.
